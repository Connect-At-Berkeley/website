Why Donate?

Connect@Cal is proud to be a stepping stone in student’s success on and off-campus. By providing a wide range of resources to students, we are helping them find their home in such an immense campus. As an organization, we strive to expand our services to every student at UC Berkeley, yet we do not have sufficient funds to accomplish this. Thus, we hope to acquire additional funds from the generosity of friends, family, alumni, and other supporters.
Your donation will be directly spent on outreach and operation expenses. We have received great feedback on our service and would love to spread it to even more of the campus. Alongside this, we have invested in numerous integration platforms that have allowed us to increase the efficiency and quality of our resource responses.
Please consider donating to our organization to continue supporting our fellow Cal bears! All donations to the Connect At Berkeley are tax-deductible as allowed by law.*

*Connect At Berkeley is a sponsored student group of Associated Students of the University of California, a non-profit unincorporated association, IRS Code 501c3. Your donation to the ASUC on behalf of ASUC/Connect At Berkeley may be claimed as charitable. Documentation of the ASUC's Tax Identification or a tax deductible receipt is available upon request.
